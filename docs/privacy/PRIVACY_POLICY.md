# Privacy Policy for StillOnTime

**Last Updated:** January 2025

## 1. Introduction

StillOnTime ("we," "our," or "us") is a film schedule automation service that helps film industry professionals manage their shooting schedules, calculate optimal routes, and receive weather forecasts. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly
- **Account Information:** Your name and email address when you create an account
- **Configuration Data:** Home address, Panavision address, and notification preferences
- **Communication Preferences:** SMS phone numbers, push notification tokens, and email preferences

### 2.2 Information We Collect Through Google OAuth
When you authenticate with Google, we collect:
- **Profile Information:** Your Google ID, email address, and display name
- **Access Tokens:** Secure tokens to access your Google services (Gmail, Calendar, Drive)
- **Required Scopes:** We request access to:
  - Gmail (read-only access to detect schedule emails)
  - Google Calendar (to create shooting schedule events)
  - Google Drive (to access PDF attachments)
  - Basic profile information (email and name)

### 2.3 Information We Process from Your Emails
- **Email Metadata:** Subject lines, sender information, and timestamps
- **PDF Attachments:** Schedule documents containing:
  - Shooting dates and call times
  - Location information
  - Scene details and equipment lists
  - Safety notes and contact information
- **Processing Status:** Whether emails have been processed and any errors encountered

### 2.4 Location and Route Information
- **Addresses:** Your configured home and work addresses for route calculation
- **Route Data:** Calculated travel times, departure times, and route segments
- **Geographic Data:** Location coordinates for weather forecasting

### 2.5 Weather and Calendar Data
- **Weather Forecasts:** Temperature, precipitation, and weather warnings for shooting locations
- **Calendar Events:** Created events with your shooting schedules and route information
- **Notifications:** SMS and email notifications about schedules and weather

### 2.6 Technical Information
- **Logs:** Application logs, error reports, and performance metrics
- **Device Information:** Browser type, operating system, and device identifiers
- **Usage Analytics:** How you interact with our service (pages visited, features used)

## 3. How We Use Your Information

### 3.1 Primary Service Functions
- **Schedule Processing:** Automatically detect and parse shooting schedule emails
- **Route Planning:** Calculate optimal departure times and travel routes
- **Calendar Integration:** Create calendar events with your shooting schedules
- **Weather Monitoring:** Provide weather forecasts for shooting locations
- **Notifications:** Send SMS, email, and push notifications about your schedules

### 3.2 Service Improvement
- **Analytics:** Understand how our service is used to improve functionality
- **Error Handling:** Debug and fix technical issues
- **Feature Development:** Develop new features based on usage patterns

### 3.3 Legal and Safety
- **Compliance:** Meet legal obligations and regulatory requirements
- **Security:** Protect against fraud, abuse, and security threats
- **Communication:** Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support

## 4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

### 4.1 Third-Party Services
We share information with the following services to provide our functionality:

**Google Services:**
- **Gmail API:** To monitor your emails for shooting schedules
- **Google Calendar API:** To create calendar events
- **Google Drive API:** To access PDF attachments
- **Google Maps API:** To calculate routes and geocode locations

**External Services:**
- **OpenWeatherMap:** To provide weather forecasts
- **Twilio:** To send SMS notifications (if enabled)

### 4.2 Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law or to:
- Comply with legal processes or government requests
- Protect our rights, property, or safety
- Protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or others
- Investigate fraud or security issues

### 4.3 Business Transfers
In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

### 4.4 We Do Not Sell Your Data
We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

## 5. Data Security

### 5.1 Security Measures
We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:
- **Encryption:** All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- **Access Controls:** Strict access controls and authentication requirements
- **Regular Audits:** Security audits and vulnerability assessments
- **Data Minimization:** We collect only the information necessary for our services

### 5.2 Google OAuth Security
- **Secure Tokens:** OAuth tokens are encrypted and stored securely
- **Token Refresh:** Automatic token refresh to maintain security
- **Scope Limitation:** We request only the minimum required permissions

## 6. Data Retention

### 6.1 Retention Periods
- **User Accounts:** Retained until you delete your account
- **Email Data:** Processed emails and schedules retained for 2 years
- **Route Plans:** Retained for 1 year for historical analysis
- **Weather Data:** Retained for 6 months
- **Logs:** Retained for 90 days for security and debugging purposes

### 6.2 Data Deletion
You can request deletion of your data at any time. We will delete your information within 30 days of your request, except where we are required to retain it for legal or regulatory purposes.

### 7.1 Access and Control

### 7.2 Google OAuth Controls
